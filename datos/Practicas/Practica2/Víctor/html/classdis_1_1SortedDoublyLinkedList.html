Deletes the given position. Time analysis: O(n).

  • [in] position is the position to be deleted.
    Precondition
    not isEmpty()
    Postcondition
    savedCurrent.isLast(position) implies not isValid(position)
    position=0 and savedCurrent.isLast(position) implies isEmpty()
    not savedCurrent.isLast(position) implies getItem(position)=savedCurrent.item(position+1)
